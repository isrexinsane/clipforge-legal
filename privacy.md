# Privacy Policy

**ClipForge — GIF Maker**
*Last updated: April 22, 2026*

## Overview

ClipForge ("the App") is developed by Ronin Art House. This privacy policy explains how the App handles your information.

## Information We Collect

**We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.**

The App operates as follows:

- **Clipboard access:** The App reads your device clipboard to detect social media URLs when you open the App. This data is processed locally on your device and is not transmitted to any server other than the video extraction service described below.
- **Video extraction:** When you import a video, the App sends the social media URL to our backend server to retrieve the video file. The URL is processed in real time and is not stored after the request completes.
- **GIF creation:** All GIF encoding happens locally on your device. Your created GIFs are saved to your device's photo library. We do not have access to your GIFs.
- **Photo library access:** The App requests permission to save GIFs to your photo library. This access is used solely for saving your created GIFs. We do not read, upload, or access any other photos or videos in your library.

## Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services to extract video from social media platforms:

- **RapidAPI** — for Instagram video extraction
- **Railway** — for hosting our backend extraction service

These services process the social media URL you provide but do not receive any personal information.

## Data Storage

- No user accounts are created
- No personal data is stored on our servers
- No analytics or tracking tools are used
- No cookies are used

## In-App Purchases

The App offers an optional annual subscription for premium features. Subscriptions are processed entirely through Apple's App Store. We do not have access to your payment information.

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The App is not directed at children.
